# VibeBox 🎵

A mood-based music recommender system that curates playlists based on your current vibe, powered by AI.

## 🛠 Features
* **Mood-based Curation:** Get music recommendations based on your current mood.
* **Category & Language filters:** Browse music by categories or your favorite languages.
* **AI-Powered:** Uses advanced AI to match songs with your specific vibe.
* **Playlist Management:** Create, view, and manage your custom music collections.

## 💻 Tech Stack
* **Framework:** Next.js 14+
* **Language:** JavaScript
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **Deployment:** Netlify
